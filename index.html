What We Do

Our mission is to provide personalized support and create inclusive, engaging experiences for children with autism, while offering UofT students meaningful volunteer opportunities.

1-on-1 Mentorship

We pair UofT student mentors with children to build supportive friendships, focusing on shared interests and goals.

Group Events

From sensory-friendly craft sessions to inclusive sports days, we host events that are fun for the whole family.

Community Support

We aim to build a strong community for parents, mentors, and children, offering resources and a network of support.

About Our Club

Founded in 2023, the Autism Educational Support Club (AESC) at the University of Toronto is a student-run organization dedicated to bridging the gap between the UofT community and the local autism community. We believe in the power of connection, support, and education to make a meaningful difference.

Our Mission

Our mission is twofold:

  • To provide children with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) with dedicated mentors who support their social, educational, and personal growth.
  • To offer University of Toronto students invaluable volunteer experience, fostering empathy, leadership, and a deep understanding of neurodiversity.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every child with autism has the opportunity to thrive, supported by a network of compassionate and dedicated individuals. We strive to create an inclusive environment at UofT and beyond, where neurodiversity is celebrated.
